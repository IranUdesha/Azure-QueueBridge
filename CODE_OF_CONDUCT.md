# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

1. Demonstrating empathy and kindness.
2. Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences.
3. Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
4. Taking responsibility and learning from mistakes.
5. Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

1. The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances.
2. Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
3. Public or private harassment.
4. Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
5. Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in
response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, and also applies when
an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the maintainers through repository discussions or issues.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
